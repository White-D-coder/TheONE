# Engineer OS: Product Vision & Core Principles

## Vision Statement
Engineer OS is the definitive personal execution system for ambitious engineers. It transforms potential into measurable, compounding career value by enforcing discipline, tracking skills, and generating public proof of competence.

## Target Audience
College students and early-career engineers aiming for elite performance (FAANG, high-growth startups, research, or solo-founding).

## Core Principles
1. **Proof Over Intention**: We value what you build, not what you plan.
2. **Adaptive Over Static**: Your routine must breathe with your life (exams, sprints, burnout).
3. **Compounding Over Intensity**: 1% better every day beats a 100% burst once a month.
4. **Evidence-Driven Worth**: Your "market value" is a function of verifiable output.
5. **Human in the Loop**: AI drafts and suggests; humans approve and execute.

## Success Metrics
- **Consistency**: Streak of non-negotiable daily habits.
- **Skill Velocity**: Rate of progress in core technical/communication domains.
- **Proof Count**: Number of public artifacts (commits, posts, articles).
- **Opportunity Fit**: Alignment of discovered roles with current skill levels.
- **Monthly Worth**: A calculated score representing professional leverage.
