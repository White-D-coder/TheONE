# Engineer OS: Public Proof Engine & Content Studio

## Purpose
To turn private work into visible credibility. If the world doesn't see it, it didn't happen (in market terms).

## The Content Studio
A workspace to draft, refine, and queue public updates.
### Supported Formats:
- **LinkedIn Posts**: Professional updates, project launches.
- **Technical Threads**: Deep dives into architecture or bugs.
- **Article Outlines**: Structuring longer pieces for Medium or Blogs.
- **GitHub READMEs**: Polishing project documentation.

## The Proof Pipeline
1. **Input**: Evidence from the Vault (e.g., a new feature).
2. **Drafting**: AI generates 3 options (Strict, Excited, Educational).
3. **Refinement**: User edits and approves.
4. **Visibility Strategy**: AI suggests the best platform and timing for the post.

## Principles of Proof
- **No Cringe**: Avoid empty hype and "I'm humbled to announce" fluff. Focus on "Here is what I built and why it matters."
- **High Signal**: Every post must teach something or show something tangible.
- **Consistency**: Maintaining a steady "Proof Stream" rather than periodic bursts.
