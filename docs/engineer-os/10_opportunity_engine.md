# Engineer OS: Opportunity Discovery Engine

## Purpose
To match the user's current "Worth" and "Skills" with high-value career opportunities.

## Discovery Logic
The Engine filters opportunities (Internships, Jobs, Freelance, Hackathons) based on:
- **Skill Fit**: Do you have the evidence to back up the requirements?
- **Upside**: Will this role significantly boost your Monthly Worth?
- **ROI**: Is the application cost worth the potential reward?

## Application Assistant
When a user decides to apply:
1. **Resume Tailoring**: AI selects relevant Evidence and Skills to highlight.
2. **Cover Letter Draft**: Focuses on "Proof of Work" rather than "Interest".
3. **Interview Pointers**: "They use React and AWS. Review these 3 projects in your Vault before the call."

## Tracking State
- **Discovered**: New roles found by the engine.
- **Shortlisted**: Saved by the user.
- **Submitted**: Active applications.
- **Interview**: Ongoing negotiation.
- **Result**: Accepted/Rejected/Ghosted (Used to adjust future Discovery logic).
