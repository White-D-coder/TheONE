# Engineer OS: Monthly Worth Calculator

## Purpose
To provide a non-subjective estimate of the user's career leverage and market value.

## The Formula (Conceptual)
`Monthly Worth = (Output * 0.3) + (SkillGrowth * 0.2) + (PublicProof * 0.2) + (Consistency * 0.2) + (OpportunityFlow * 0.1)`

### Breakdown:
- **Output**: Volume and quality of shipped code/projects.
- **Skill Growth**: Delta in technical and speaking scores.
- **Public Proof**: Engagement and credibility of public artifacts.
- **Consistency**: Adherence to non-negotiables (no zero days).
- **Opportunity Flow**: Quality and quantity of interview invites or offers.

## The Review Process
At the end of every month, the OS generates a **Worth Report**:
1. **Total Score**: A number from 0 to 1000.
2. **Delta**: Growth compared to last month.
3. **Trajectory**: "At this rate, you'll be ready for Senior roles in 14 months."
4. **Strategy Shift**: "Your technical output is high, but your visibility is zero. Shift weights to Public Proof next month."

## Why?
The Worth Score isn't "Truth", but it's a **useful compass**. It prevents the "Tutorial Hell" trap and forces the user to focus on high-leverage activities.
